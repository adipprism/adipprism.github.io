About the Organizing Committee

The organizing committee for this event is made up of students ranging from grades eight to twelve, with various talents and positive attributes. Executives for the organizing committee were chosen by members of the TDSB SuperCouncil. Ambassadors were carefully selected by a recruitment team comprised of members of the executive team.

About SuperCouncil

SuperCouncil is a democratically elected student body in the TDSB that allows the student voice to be heard in the TDSB boardroom. They host many events such as “Student Leadership Day”, for student leaders from all over the board to meet and bond with each other.

Organized by the Area D Initiative Planning Committee and the TDSB SuperCouncil

About PRISM

Class it up, bring a friend and enjoy a night filled with pieces designed to inform and inspire. This May 13th, Supercouncil Area D is hosting an art social, consisting of live performances and student made art made to bring awareness to mental health and the mental illnesses that affect 10-20% of all Canadian youth. This networking event will give you the opportunity to meet and mingle with students from across Toronto. *Come dressed up and open minded, leave aware and with newly acquainted with awesome people.
